FAQ.

Frequently Asked Questions.

How long does the process take from start to finish?
We generally finish a website from start to finish within 2 weeks and is all dependent on how soon clients respond to our content and image requests as well as the availability of our designers and developers.
Do you provide support after the website is launched?
Yes, we offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website runs smoothly.
Can you redesign my existing website?
Yes, we specialize in website redesigns to enhance user experience and modernize your online presence.
Do you provide hosting services?
Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions as part of our web design packages, ensuring your website is secure and accessible.
What platforms do you use for web development?
We use WordPress for building, Cloudways for hosting (comes with SSL certificate), and Figma for design.
Why WordPress over custom code? What are the advantages?
WordPress offers a reliable, flexible, and cost-effective solution that fits most business needs. Unlike custom-coded websites, which require more time and resources to build and maintain, WordPress allows us to create a fully functional, professional website within 2 weeks! It has thousands of plugins and themes, enabling us to customize the design and features to perfectly match your brand. Additionally, WordPress is built with SEO in mind, regularly updated for security, and scalable, so your site can grow seamlessly alongside your business.
